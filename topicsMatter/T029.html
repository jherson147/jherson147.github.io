b) (r^2 +3r )/(1-r^2)

c) (r^2 + 1)/(1-r^2)

d) ( 3r + 1)/(1-r^2)

a) 2/3

b) 4

c) 25/49

d) 1/2

a) P/2

b) P

c) P/4

d) 3/4P
